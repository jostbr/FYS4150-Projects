
To reproduce the benchmark files in this directory, run main.cpp as following:
- earth_2body.txt --> scenario = "2Body", t_max = 15 [yr], t_write = 4 [days], h = 0.1 [days]
- sun_3bodyfull.txt --> scenario = "3BodyFull", t_max = 15 [yr], t_write = 4 [days], h = 0.1 [days]
- earth_3bodyfull.txt --> scenario = "3BodyFull", t_max = 15 [yr], t_write = 4 [days], h = 0.1 [days]
- jupiter_3bodyfull.txt --> scenario = "3BodyFull", t_max = 15 [yr], t_write = 4 [days], h = 0.1 [days]
